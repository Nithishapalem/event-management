Haldi

Turmeric, known as haldi in Hindi, is a vibrant yellow spice derived from the Curcuma longa plant. It contains a compound called curcumin, which has powerful anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

Mangalasnanam

The word “Mangala” means pure, and “Snanam” means bath. Thus, Mangalasnanam means “purifying bath.” This is the first ritual conducted on the day of the wedding. A paste of sandalwood, turmeric, and saffron is prepared and applied to the bride's face and body.
